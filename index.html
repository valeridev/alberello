Alberello Restaurant

Welcome to Alberello, a hidden treasure nestled in the heart of Bexley Village. Step into a world where the timeless allure of Italian traditions intertwines with the finest British culinary delights. At Alberello, we take great pride in curating a menu that celebrates the authentic essence of Italian cuisine while also embracing the rich flavors of British favorites. Prepare to embark on a gastronomic journey like no other. Savor the succulence of our Roast Leg of British Lamb, a hearty and cherished Sunday roast that pays homage to the best of British culinary heritage. Each tender morsel is lovingly prepared, ensuring a symphony of flavors that will leave you longing for more. Delight in the exquisite taste of our Fresh Dover Sole, a delectable dish that brings the essence of the sea to your plate. Our skilled chefs artfully prepare this delicate fish, allowing the natural flavors to shine, and creating a culinary experience that is simply unparalleled. To complement your meal, our wine selection is a masterpiece in itself, with wines carefully chosen to enhance every dish and suit every palate. From classic Italian varietals to exquisite British labels, our collection promises to elevate your dining experience to new heights. At Alberello, we invite you to savor the best of both worlds. Join us as we marry Italian traditions with the finest British ingredients, creating an unforgettable dining experience that will captivate your senses and leave you with cherished memories. We look forward to welcoming you to our warm and inviting restaurant, where the flavors of Italy and Britain dance harmoniously on your palate. Buon appetito and cheers to a delightful culinary adventure!
